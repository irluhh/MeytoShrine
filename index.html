    Perception & Facts

    In the past, the Meyto Shrine in Calumpit, Bulacan was known mostly by local residents as a holy and historical place believed to be where the first Catholic Mass in the province took place. Although it had great spiritual and cultural importance, it was not widely recognized beyond the town because of limited publicity and support from the government. The shrine also faced issues with maintenance and lacked proper facilities. Today, peopleâ€™s perception of Meyto Shrine has improved as it gains more attention from both the community and local authorities. It is now being promoted as a cultural and religious heritage site, with efforts to improve its facilities, add signs, and include it in school and tourism activities. At present, the shrine is valued not only as a sacred site but also as an important historical landmark that reflects the roots of Christianity and the rich culture of Bulacan.
